Heritage Academy soccer has a proud tradition of excellence, with multiple league championships and tournament appearances. Our program emphasizes skill development, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

## Program Highlights

- Independent School League Champions (2022, 2023)
- NEPSAC Tournament Qualifiers
- College placement assistance for student-athletes

## Training Philosophy

We believe in developing well-rounded student-athletes who excel both on the field and in the classroom. Our training program balances technical skill development, tactical understanding, and physical conditioning.
