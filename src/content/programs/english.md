The English Department at Heritage Academy fosters a love of language and literature while developing essential skills in critical thinking, analysis, and communication.

## Curriculum Highlights

Our curriculum spans classic and contemporary literature, with a strong emphasis on writing across genres. Students engage in seminar-style discussions that encourage deep textual analysis and the development of original ideas.

### Lower School

- Introduction to Literary Analysis
- Creative Writing Fundamentals
- Grammar and Composition

### Upper School

- American Literature
- British Literature
- Advanced Placement English Literature
- Advanced Placement English Language
- Creative Writing Workshop
- Journalism

## The Harkness Method

Following the tradition of discussion-based learning, our English classes utilize the Harkness method, where students gather around an oval table to engage in collaborative inquiry and debate.
