The Mathematics Department prepares students for success in higher education and careers requiring quantitative skills through rigorous coursework and individualized instruction.

## Course Offerings

Our math program meets students where they are and challenges them to reach their full potential. From foundational algebra to advanced calculus and statistics, we offer courses at every level.

### Core Sequence

- Algebra I & II
- Geometry
- Precalculus
- Calculus (AB and BC)

### Advanced & Elective Courses

- Multivariable Calculus
- Linear Algebra
- Statistics and Probability
- Advanced Topics in Mathematics

## Math Lab

Our Math Lab provides drop-in tutoring and support for students at all levels, staffed by faculty and trained peer tutors.
