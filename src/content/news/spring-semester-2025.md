We are thrilled to welcome students back to campus for the spring semester of 2025. This term brings new courses, performances, athletic competitions, and opportunities for community engagement.

## Highlights This Semester

### Academic Programs

Our new interdisciplinary course, "Climate Science and Policy," will launch this semester, bringing together faculty from the science, history, and economics departments.

### Arts & Performance

The spring musical, "Into the Woods," will be performed in March. Auditions are now open to all students.

### Athletics

Winter athletic teams are off to a strong start, with several teams ranked in the top ten of their respective leagues.

## Important Dates

- January 15: Classes begin
- February 17-21: Winter break
- March 10-14: Arts Week
- April 5-12: Spring break
- May 23: Commencement

We look forward to a wonderful semester together.
