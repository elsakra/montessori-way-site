Dr. Sarah Morrison has served as Head of School since 2018, bringing over 25 years of experience in independent school education. Under her leadership, Heritage Academy has strengthened its commitment to academic excellence while expanding programs in the arts, athletics, and community service.

Prior to Heritage, Dr. Morrison served as Dean of Faculty at Phillips Academy Andover and as an English teacher and department chair at Milton Academy.
