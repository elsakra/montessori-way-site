---
question: What are the application requirements for admission?
category: Admissions
order: 1
---

Our application requires:

1. **Online Application Form** - Complete through our admissions portal
2. **School Transcripts** - Official transcripts from current and previous schools
3. **Standardized Testing** - SSAT or ISEE scores
4. **Teacher Recommendations** - Two recommendations from current teachers (English and Math)
5. **Student Essay** - Personal statement
6. **Interview** - On-campus or virtual interview with admissions

Financial aid applicants must also submit the Parents' Financial Statement (PFS) through School and Student Services (SSS).
